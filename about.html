BBE

B.A. (Hons.) Business Economics, is 1 of 3 courses which was locked behind DUJAT, before CUET was introduced.

This brief history tells us that this is one of the courses, which even the UOD deemed to deserve an examination for admission in this, as when only 12th Grade marks were considered for admission in UOD.

Now even in CUET the marking scheme is different for these 3 courses(BBE, BMS & BFIA), which is one of the most versatile in CUET 2026 (Language + Maths + General Aptitude Test + One subject of choice). Let me remind you this combination is one of the most difficult in view of the student.

Inside BBE
